Our story

FlashJoy started with a simple idea: make it effortless for hosts to create moments guests actually remember. We obsessed over flattering lighting, snappy sharing, and a service that just works — so you can enjoy your event.

Today we capture smiles across weddings, birthdays, corporate parties and pop-ups. We bring the pro kit, the props, and the good vibes — then deliver a beautiful gallery the next day.

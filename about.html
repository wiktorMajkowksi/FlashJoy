About us

FlashJoy was created to make photo booth hire feel simple, stylish and reliable. We focus on clean setups, flattering lighting, instant prints and a smooth guest experience from start to finish.

Whether it's a wedding, birthday, prom, corporate event or private party, we bring the booth, set it up, help guests use it and deliver the digital gallery after the event.
